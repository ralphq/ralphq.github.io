Sauron

(HackPSU AI and Overall Best Hack Winner, April 2022)

Sauron is a data pipeline for the collection and distribution of information about humanitarian crises in real time It uses computer vision (security cameras, satellite imagery, etc.), to upload the locations of dangerous events to a map in real time. As a minimum viable product, our team built out the infrastructure required for data collection from two sources; real-time traffic camera feeds and an app that allowed users to upload images manually (they would then be automatically geotagged and displayed). After 22 hours of work and 2 hours of sleep (thankfully) we won the best overall hack prize, as well as the Nittany Ai prize for best hack that utilized artificial intelligence.
