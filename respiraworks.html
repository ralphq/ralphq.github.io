Mechanical Engineer

Respiraworks Inc.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, a worldwide shortage of ventilators caused life-and-death logistical problems for hospitals with too many patients who required breathing assistance. This led to the founding of Respiraworks, a non-profit building low-cost, open source ventilators for use in the third world (mainly South America).

Working in biomedical technology taught me the importance of iterative design, objective evaluation of alternative methods to solve problems, and relevant, testable system requirements. Furthermore, I faced some unique challenges I have not since encountered in aerospace, like working on a global and multi-lingual team as well as cost minimization.
