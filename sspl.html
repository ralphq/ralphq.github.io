Student Training Program

Student Space Programs Lab (SSPL)

For this training program, students were put in teams of 3 and tasked with the design, manufacture, testing, and actual launch of a telemetry payload for model rockets. I designed and manufactured the payload housing, which served as my introduction to Solidworks, selected & purchased electronic components, and soldered said components to an Arduino microcontroller. Using an IMU, altimeter, and even a pressure sensor our team was able to fuse the data into a continuous flight trajectory which we presented to other teams at the end of the program.
