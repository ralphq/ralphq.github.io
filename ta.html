Wargame Modelling & Simulation Engineer

True Anomaly

True Anomaly builds technologies at the intersection of aerospace, software, and AI engineering to preserve American space superiority. These technologies include Jackal, an autonomous orbital pursuit vehicle capable of imaging adversarial spacecraft and contesting orbits in LEO, GEO and even Cislunar space, and Mosaic, an AI-powered operating system enabling operators to manage space threats and assets, fuse data from disparate, heterogeneous sources, and even pilot spacecraft.

As a Wargame Modelling & Simulation Engineer, I'm working with the company's strategy department to develop applications that help train operators, validate mission architectures, and experiment with potential new systems. I'm incredibly grateful to be part of a team pushing the boundaries of aerospace and national security and to do work involving several fields I'm passionate about, including modelling & simulation, spacecraft GNC, and AI.
